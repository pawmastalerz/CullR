# Privacy Policy

CullR does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Data access

- Photos and videos are accessed locally on your device to enable swiping,
  previewing, and deletion.
- All processing stays on-device.

## External links

- The app includes a link to Buy Me a Coffee. If you open it, your browser may
  share data with that site under their privacy policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about privacy, open an issue in this repository.
